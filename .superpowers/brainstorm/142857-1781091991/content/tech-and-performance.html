2. Performance Optimization

Frustum Culling

Only render 3D objects that are currently within the camera's view, saving GPU cycles during fly-throughs.

LOD (Level of Detail)

Use low-poly models for objects far away on the spline, swapping to high-fidelity "inspection" models as the camera approaches.

3. The Interaction Model

To keep the site accessible, we will include a "Tactical Override" button that allows users to switch back to a standard 2D grid if they prefer a more traditional browsing experience.
